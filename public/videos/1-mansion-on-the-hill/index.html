Mansion on the Hill

The very first lockdown jam we recorded, Mansion On The Hill from Neil Young’s Ragged Glory, set the tone for what became a unique and rewarding project. Capturing the raw, rugged Crazy Horse sound while working remotely from our own homes was no small feat, but somehow, we managed to create a live vibe that feels authentic to the original. We still remember how surreal it was piecing everything together—each of us recording in isolation yet somehow syncing up as if we were in the same room. This jam holds a special place for us, marking the start of something truly creative during those unusual times.
